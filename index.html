About Me

My Story

Hello! My name is Blake Trester and I am currently a student enrolled in the UT Austin coding bootcamp. When I finish with this class, I will be a full stack web developer based out of Austin, TX. In 2011, I graduated from TSTC Waco with an Associates Degree of Applied Science in Digital Media Design. Coming from my background in graphic design, I love working with strong visual elements to bring people's visions to life. After learning gaining a vast skillset in media design, I decided I wanted to continue my education and learn about the more technical side of development. My design expertise allows me to better bridge the gap between developers and designers for a stronger collaboration among the team. I am always looking to challenge myself and grow as a developer, I see any chance I have to learn something new as an amazing opportunity. If you think we would be a good fit please reach out!
